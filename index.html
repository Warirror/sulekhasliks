Our Story

Born in
Kerala's Looms

Sulekha Silks was founded in 1978 by Sulekha Menon, a weaver's daughter from Thrissur who believed that the finest silks deserve to reach every home — not just palatial ones. What began as a small loom-house has grown into Kerala's most trusted silk brand.

Every saree, every metre of silk we sell carries the fingerprints of our master weavers — men and women who have dedicated their lives to this ancient art. We partner with artisan families across Thrissur, Kanchipuram, and Chanderi to bring you silks of unmatched quality.

Our Craft

From Loom to Your Home

01
Source the Silk

We source only Grade-A pure silk threads from trusted sericulture farms, handpicked by our quality team each season.

02
Dye & Prepare

Threads are dyed in small batches using traditional and AZO-free colourants for vivid, lasting, wash-safe colour.

03
Weave by Hand

Master weavers work on traditional pit-looms, creating intricate zari patterns — some taking up to 2 weeks per saree.

04
Deliver with Care

Each piece is tagged with the weaver's name, wrapped in muslin, and shipped to your door. Cash on delivery — always.
